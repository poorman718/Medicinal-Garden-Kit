Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Medicinal Garden Kit

Do I need gardening experience to grow these herbs?

Not at all! Our Medicinal Garden Kit is designed specifically for beginners. The included guide provides simple, step-by-step instructions for planting and caring for each herb. Most of these plants are quite hardy and easy to grow with minimal care.

How much space do I need to grow these herbs?

You can grow all 10 herbs in as little as 4 square feet by planting them in containers on a patio or balcony. If you have garden space, they'll thrive even more. Many customers successfully grow these indoors near sunny windows too.

What climate zones are these herbs suitable for?

The herbs in our kit are selected to grow well in most climate zones (USDA zones 3-9). Some are annuals that grow in any zone for one season, while others are perennials that return year after year in appropriate zones. The guide includes specific care instructions for different climates.

How long until I can harvest and use my herbs?

Most herbs will be ready for light harvesting in 60-90 days after planting. Some fast-growing herbs like chamomile and lemon balm may be ready sooner. The guide specifies when and how to harvest each plant for optimal medicinal properties.

What if my seeds don't grow?

We stand behind our products with a 365-day satisfaction guarantee. If you have any issues with germination or growth, simply contact us and we'll replace your seeds or refund your money - your choice. Our seeds have a 90%+ germination rate when properly planted.
